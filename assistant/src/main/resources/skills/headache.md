For a mild headache:

- Rest in a quiet place.
- Drink fluids.
- Avoid alcohol.
- Consider whether sleep, stress, skipped meals, dehydration, or screen strain may be involved.
- The user may consider usual over-the-counter pain relief only if it is safe for them and they normally tolerate it.

Recommend medical care for sudden worst headache, neurological symptoms, fever with stiff neck, head injury, pregnancy, persistent or worsening pain, or a headache that feels unusual for the user.

Do not diagnose the cause of the headache with certainty.
