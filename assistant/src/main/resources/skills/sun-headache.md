For headache after sun, heat, or possible dehydration exposure:

- Move to a cool shaded place.
- Rest and avoid more sun or heat.
- Drink water or oral rehydration fluids if the user can drink safely.
- Consider light food if they have not eaten.
- Avoid alcohol.

Recommend urgent medical care if there is confusion, fainting, very high fever, repeated vomiting, severe or worsening headache, stiff neck, weakness, trouble breathing, or symptoms do not improve.

Do not diagnose heat stroke or prescribe medication. Encourage a licensed clinician for persistent or concerning symptoms.
