I can give general health guidance, but I cannot diagnose you or replace a licensed clinician.

Helpful next steps:

- Notice how long the symptom has been happening and whether it is improving or worsening.
- Rest and avoid activities that clearly make it worse.
- Drink fluids and eat normally if you can.
- Watch for new symptoms such as fever, severe pain, spreading swelling, weakness, trouble breathing, dizziness, confusion, or symptoms that feel unusual for you.

Please contact a clinician if symptoms are persistent, worsening, severe, recurrent, happen after an injury, or if you have medical conditions, pregnancy, allergies, or medications that could make the situation more complicated.

Seek urgent medical help for chest pain, trouble breathing, stroke-like symptoms, fainting, major bleeding, severe allergic reaction, severe or rapidly worsening pain, or thoughts of self-harm.
